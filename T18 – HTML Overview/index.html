Hobbies

Cycling

Cycling was an adventure every time. I would explore new streets and feel the thrill of the open road. One day, I rode too fast down a hill and fell, but I got back up and kept going.

TV Game

TV Game was my favorite way to pass the time. I loved challenging myself with different levels and competing with my friends. One of my fondest memories was finally beating a game I had struggled with for weeks.

Toys

Remote Control Car

Remote Control Car was my pride and joy. I would race it around the house and create obstacle courses. I remember getting my first high-speed RC car and feeling amazed at how fast it could go.

Die-Cast Robot

My Die-Cast Robot was one of the coolest toys I owned. It was sturdy, detailed, and had movable parts.
